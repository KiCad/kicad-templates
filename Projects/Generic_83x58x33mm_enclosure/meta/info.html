This project template is the basis of a PCB backplate board for the Generic 83x58x33 mm enclosure

This enclosure is "generic Unbranded". Lots of different manufacturers & part numbers.
Here is an example of an SZOMK branded AK10019-A1 part number.

Similar models with wall mounting tabs, thicker walls, non transparent lids, etc. are marketed
with slightly different measurements, but share the same hole distance and PCB inner space.

They are very common and easily available in online markets using the search term: "83x58x33mm enclosure".
